Design Summary

For my next design challenge I got the prompt for designing a digital event ticket for a social event in San Francisco. I wanted to create something that was visually striking while practicing my skills with a vertical composition and digital design.

The Process

I started by writing down some attributes: Digital Ticket - Futuristic, easy, simple, practical. San Francisco - Techy, upscale, progressive. From there I created some sketches as seen below for the ticket. I wanted to create something that took these above attributes into account while also playing to the vertical layout of the composition.

Sketches

Final

For the final I combined a few of my sketches together after bringing them to the computer. I wanted to go with a familiar color scheme (San Francisco Giants) to create something recognizable and eye-catching. Originally, the text on the background of a blurred image did not look great, so I went with the coffee cup pouring out to show some movement and afford myself the ability to play with the overall hierarchy of the piece. All in all I am very happy with the way this project worked out. This was one of my later projects in the design challenge run I did back in 2022, and I think it is clear to see how each project improves from the last.
